Privacy Policy

Welcome to Deal Way. We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

To provide our services, we may collect the following information:

  • Pi Account Info: We collect your Pi Network username and Wallet Address to facilitate payments and identification within the app.
  • User Content: Details of the ads you post, including product images, descriptions, and prices.
  • Communication Data: Messages exchanged between you and other users via the internal chat system.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the app to improve user experience.

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To enable you to post ads and communicate with buyers/sellers.
  • To facilitate and verify Pi payments (Testnet/Mainnet).
  • To improve app performance and develop new features.
  • To prevent fraud and misuse and ensure community security.

3. Sharing Information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following cases:

  • With other users to the extent necessary to complete transactions (e.g., showing your username and product image).
  • To comply with legal requirements or respond to official requests if necessary.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and your continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of those changes.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the support channels available in the app.
